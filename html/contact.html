About

Arch Demo Ltd. is an international architectural practice based in Athens, Greece and founded in 1943. The studio provides comprehensive services in building design, interior design, contracting and project management. The firm has completed a wide spectrum of projects of programs scales and locations, as London, Paris, Athens, Dubai and so on. Projects range in scale from complete buildings to interiors for private apartments, corporate offices, retail stores, restaurants, boats and architectural detailing.
