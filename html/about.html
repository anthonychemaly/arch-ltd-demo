What Do We Do?



Arch Demo Ltd. is an international architectural practice based in Athens, Greece and founded in 1943.
The studio provides comprehensive services in building design, interior design, contracting and project management. The firm has completed a wide spectrum of projects of programs scales and locations, as London, Paris, Athens, Dubai and so on.
Projects range in scale from complete buildings to interiors for private apartments, corporate offices, retail stores, restaurants, boats and architectural detailing.

Meet Our Team

John Smith

Founder & Partner


John Smith was a self-taught sculptor, selling his works to cover his education since the age of 19. Working part-time, he received his Bachelor of Fine Arts from UCL. He then joined a design office before founding his own design practice and workshop in 1970.
The reason for establishing a workshop with the office is to constantly experiment with new materials, forms and structure into offering innovative pieces but also to assure high-end quality of finishing and detailing.
John Smith worked on large scale residential projects in Paris, London and Dropmore (UK), Athens and Vouliagmeni, Doha, Dubai.
Furthermore he has designed boat interiors and has done product designs for his residential projects, covering furniture to accessories.


Lily Doe

Partner


Lily Doe received her Master in Fine Arts in Interior Architecture from DKPS being offered a full scholarship for her years of study. After graduation she joined AD as intern and climbed the ladder to senior positions. Her works covered projects, in Paris, Athens and Beirut, across different scales and types from residential to retail.


Tony-John Smith

Managing Director


Tony-John is a graduate of University College London (UCL) where he received with distinction a Masters of Science in Construction Finance & Management. Prior to that he graduated with a Masters in Architecture from ABC. After graduation in 2017 he founded a successful design startup which managed to earn over 100,000 in revenue in less than a year of launching.
